Checkpoint Learning provides modules to help you to develop your understanding and increase your knowledge and skills in relation to core technical areas. Each module is supported with;

Reference Materials - This defines the objective and reading material for this topic.

Interactive Test - Provides an interactive question and answer evaluation of your knowledge on the subject area.

Once completed, the test can be saved to My Folders, or you can print, email or export the results. The test can also be saved to My Folders prior to completion, so that you can return to the test at a later stage.
