Recurring employee declarations
[3-3870] 
Overview
Where an employee receives a series of benefits that are essentially the same but for the difference in the value of the benefit or the portion of business use of the benefit, a declaration will generally only need to be provided by the employee for the first benefit. A further declaration may, however, be required where:
  • the recurring benefits are provided five years after the last declaration;
  • there is a decrease in the business use of the benefit by the employee of more than 10%.
Refer to [3-3600] for the approved format of a recurring fringe benefit declaration.
Note: This measure is not applicable to benefits provided in respect of an employee's car. Recurring employee declarations can only be used in respect of expense, property and residual fringe benefits.
